Home Projects

I’m an engineer working on multimodal AI systems at Google.

Recently I’ve been working on Search AI Mode and Lens-related projects, where I’ve focused on improving how models understand and use visual information. That includes building and evaluating multimodal features such as visual search tools, and more recently improving quality in multimodal multi-turn interactions by ensuring visual context flows correctly across turns.

A lot of my work sits at the boundary between modeling and infrastructure — thinking about how tools are exposed to models, how visual signals are represented, and where grounding breaks in real user traffic.

Earlier, I worked on Google Maps, using large-scale GPS data to predict lane marker locations and enrich navigation data.

Current Interests

  • Tool-augmented language models
  • Grounding and retrieval in real-world systems
  • Designing reliable agent behaviors
  • Reducing friction in real user workflows
